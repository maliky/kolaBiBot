# Notes from the previous strategy fixture:
# Kolabi XBT scenario grammar:
# SCENARIO <name>: Given <market movement/window>; When <head condition/order type>; Then <tail behaviour/repeat/chain>.
# Price interval convention: use "- -5" for down by at least 5 USD, and "5 +" for up by at least 5 USD.
# Quantity convention: buy-head rows use unique odd quantities; sell-head rows use unique even quantities.
# Kraken boundary: head suffixes are stripped before REST placement. They only choose the local reference or trigger signal.
# Unsupported: M with i/l/m, ! on M/S/MT, and i/l/m on plain tail M/L fail clearly because no trigger price exists.
# Suffix map: l=last, m=mark, i=index. There is no f suffix because it is redundant with m on Kraken.
